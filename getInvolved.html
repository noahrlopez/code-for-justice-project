VOLUNTEER OPPORTUNITIES

BECOME AN ON-SITE HELPER

We are always in need of some extra hands. We host food and clothing drives & community clean-up's weekly. We also assist with resume building and job hunting, as well as housing applications. If you have any free time to contribute during the week, we would love to help get you trained and on the ground!
