Welcome To Banin Organization

"Advocating For Humanity"

Mission Statement

The Banin Organization advocates for people who are experiencing homelessness. We advocate for legislation that will be supportive of the needs of the homeless.

The Problem

The criminalization of homelessness refers to measures that prohibit life-sustaining activities such as sleeping/camping, eating, sitting, and/or asking for money/resources in public spaces. These ordinances include criminal penalties for violations of these acts.

The Solution

Our organization offers shelter and housing, counseling, rehabilitation and employment opportunities.
