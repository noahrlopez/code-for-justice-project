About The Banin Org

Who We Are

The Banin organization created by students of the Road to Hire program is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping the homeless. We are a social services organization, that provides critically-needed goods and services to homeless, without regard to race, color, creed, national origin, or religion.

Our mission

To prevent and end homelessness by creating shelter or housing, offer counseling, create a rehanilitation program for addicts, and by offering partner employment opportunities.

Our values

Respect

We believe in the inherent dignity of all people, and we celebrate every person's uniqueness and contributions to our work.

Commitment

We are resolute in our mission to build wellbeing and end homelessness for the people we serve.

Equity

We are commited to fairness and impartiality in the way we provide services and opportunities for service.

Excellence

We have high standards for every aspect of our work, continuosly raising the bar to provide the best experience and outcomes for everyone we serve.
